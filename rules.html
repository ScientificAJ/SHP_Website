Rules & Conduct

We're building something positive. Help keep the event welcoming and safe for everyone.

Be respectful

Respect all participants and organisers. We celebrate diversity and inclusion.

No plagiarism

Build your own ideas and credit your sources. Originality matters.

AI tools allowed

You may use AI tools – just disclose what you use and how in your submission.

No harmful content

No hate speech, NSFW or malicious projects. Keep it welcoming and safe.

Parental consent

If you’re a minor, make sure your parent or guardian has given consent to participate.

Violations may result in disqualification. We want this to be welcoming and safe for everyone.
